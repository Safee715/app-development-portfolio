School Manager App

School Manager is a multi-tenant school management app designed to make administration, teaching, and learning seamless for modern educational institutions.

Key Features

  • ✅ Multi-Tenant Support: Each institution operates independently, managing its own teachers, students, classes, and subjects securely.
  • ✅ Admin Controls: Add teachers, assign credentials, and manage access effortlessly.
  • ✅ Teacher Management: Add, edit, and view teacher details including enrollment dates and subjects.
  • ✅ Student & Class Management: Organize students, track attendance, and maintain structured records.
  • ✅ Secure Authentication: Only authorized users can access the system, keeping all data safe.
  • ✅ Future Media Support: Media uploads like videos and images will be supported in upcoming updates.
  • ✅ Intuitive & Clean UI: Designed for easy navigation and a smooth user experience.

Perfect for schools, colleges, and training institutes, the app ensures data security, role-based access, and efficient management for all users.
